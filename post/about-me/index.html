About ME

published on 2020-01-26

Introduce


I am Aixi Guo. I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in China. In 2019 I graduated with a degree in M.Sc. Information and Communication Technology at the University of Duisburg-Essen, with a focus in "Embedded Systems Engineering, ISE". As part of university projects and through my work as a student research assistant in the IoT-LAB, I have able to gain both theoretical and practical experience in the corresponding area.

I also gained more programming knowledge during my master thesis, in which I worked on a machine learning (using Keras/Python with ResNet) project to recognize bird species based on their song. I also built a 3D game using Unity to motivate the players to go to the forest and collect bird songs. This game combines all pieces of the product: UI, server, database, core AI algorithms and game elements. And it supports the Android/IOS mobile platform. For programming language, I am familiar with C, C++, C#, Python, Java, Javascript und web-develop language like HTML/CSS.

During my study at the university, I was a student research assistant in the IoT-LAB. There I designed a VR project for IoT called "Digital Twin". It combined a 3D-VR scene (a virtual greenhouse) for visualization of data and hardware (Raspberry Pi, Arduino, Sensors) for data collection. Any change in real-world data (such as sunshine quality) changes the virtual scene (e.g. sun movement). The user can also control the switches in the virtual scene to change the real device (e.g. on/off for light). Other than that I have designed a single-chip board with ATMega32 MCU in the IoT-LAB, which is later used as the students' projects and teaching tool. I was also familiar with the PCB design software like EGALE und the technology like SMD-soldering, circuit analysis and so on.
